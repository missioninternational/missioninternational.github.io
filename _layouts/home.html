Orphanage

Towards the end of our Mission trip to Africa our director stated that an orphange near Monrovia had children dying with no one to help. Babies would be left on her door step growing the need regularly. We have since monthly supported her amazing ministry of provision and love. We have built three buildings, outhouses and provided pigs and chickens.
